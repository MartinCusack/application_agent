# About the job

## Role Overview

We are looking for a Senior AI Consultant to join our growing AI Advisory team and help our public sector and enterprise clients to leverage the power of artificial intelligence.

As a Senior AI Consultant, you will work on a range of projects using your technical and business expertise to deliver business outcomes using AI technologies such as machine learning, deep learning, generative AI, computer vision, natural language processing and related capabilities.

Working in partnership with colleagues across FSP, you will be our customers’ trusted advisor helping develop AI strategies, identify AI use cases, designing and developing solutions, and ensuring that our AI solutions are ethical, transparent and trustworthy.

Due to the nature of this role successful candidates will need to undergo security clearance. To be eligible for this role you must have lived in the UK for at least 5 consecutive years. More information on SC Clearance checks can be found here. 


## Responsibilities

    Lead and manage engagements, partnering with clients to understand their requirements and challenges, and identify AI use cases and opportunities
    Create AI strategies and run maturity assessments for clients
    Design and architect AI solutions using established processes and best practices
    Build and maintain strong relationships with clients, serving as a trusted advisor and subject matter expert in AI
    Collaborate with internal teams and external partners to deliver high-quality, integrated AI solutions that meet client needs
    Communicate and present the results and insights of AI solutions to clients and stakeholders
    Actively contribute to the strategic development our AI strategy and capability
    Drive thought leadership and innovation across FSP and our clients, staying up-to-date with emerging trends, technologies, and best practices in AI
    Be a proponent of the principles and best practices of responsible AI


## About you

    At least 3 years of client facing experience working and leading on AI-led initiatives, with a strong track record of delivering measurable results
    Strong AI technical knowledge in AI solution providers’ services including, but not limited to Microsoft, AWS, Google, Open AI etc
    Skilled in using different techniques and tools to design and communicate AI narratives
    Experience in using generative AI and LLM tools and frameworks
    Experience in data analysis and machine learning programming languages, such as Python, R, SQL, Java etc.
    AI consultancy experience supporting presales and bids
    Proven stakeholder management skills, including at C-suite level
    Excellent leadership, communication, and interpersonal skills, with the ability to effectively lead teams
    Varied experience across a range of sectors and industries
