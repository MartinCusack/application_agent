## About the Role


Within this role you will have the opportunity to join our rapidly expanding Data & AI Practice as an Agentic AI Engineer. This role is positioned at the intersection of engineering, productization and AI Innovation.

You will focus on designing, building and maintaining advanced AI solutions using Microsoft Foundry (formerly Azure AI Foundry)

This is a key role in developing internal AI products, packaged as subscription-based offerings for clients, while supporting delivery projects across multiple sectors. You’ll also mentor Junior AI Engineers and help shape engineering standards, reusable components, and best practices across the AI capability.


## What you’ll be getting up to on a day-to-day basis:


    Develop, maintain, and optimise complex agentic AI solutions using Microsoft Foundry.
    Build reusable patterns, templates, and components to support the practice’s commercial AI products.
    Lead technical design for AI implementations and contribute to architecture decisions.
    Work with stakeholders to understand client problems and translate them into robust AI engineering solutions that can be standardised to a product.
    Implement LLM-based workflows, tools, agents, safety mechanisms, and evaluation pipelines.
    Ensure solutions meet security, compliance, data governance, and performance requirements.
    Mentor and support Junior AI Engineers in their progression.
    Work closely with the Data, Integration & AI leadership team to evolve capability and delivery frameworks.
